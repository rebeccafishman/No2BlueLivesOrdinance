What is The Blue Lives Matter Ordinance?

The Blue Lives Matter Ordinance makes police a protected class and anyone found guilty will be subject to additional penalties and loss of freedom

What is a Hate Crime?

A crime that violates the victim's civil rights and that is motivated by hostility to the victim's race, religion, creed, national origin, sexual orientation, or gender.
